How to use ChatSurfing

  • Type in the name the chat room you want to enter into, or click on the name of one of our "Popular chats".
  • You will then be prompted to select a username that will uniquely identify you to other participants.
  • Invite your friends to join the chat room that you have entered (send them an email, a phone message, a message on another website, etc.).
  • Start chatting with everyone in the room.
  • Click on the camera symbol beside a member's name to invite them to exchange video with you (You see each other only if they agree to exchange video)
  • If the other person accepts your invitation to exchange video, then you will see each other!
  • You can open as many chats as you like. Just click on the "Menu" symbol in the top left corner beside the ChatSurfing logo to see the option for "New chat"
  • You can open as many video sessions as you like.
  • If you wish to see who has invited you to exchange video, click on the "Menu" symbol, and select "Notifications"
